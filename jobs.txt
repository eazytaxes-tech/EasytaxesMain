## 4. US Tax Resolution Specialist

**Location:** Remote / Nashik, Maharashtra  
**Employment Type:** Full-time  
**Experience Level:** 3-6 years

### About the Role
We're seeking an experienced US Tax Resolution Specialist to help our clients resolve complex IRS issues including back taxes, audits, liens, levies, and payment plans. This role requires deep knowledge of IRS procedures, excellent negotiation skills, and the ability to represent clients before the IRS.

### Key Responsibilities
- Analyze client tax situations and develop customized resolution strategies
- Prepare and submit Offers in Compromise (OIC) applications
- Negotiate installment agreements and payment plans with the IRS
- Handle IRS audit representation and correspondence
- File penalty abatement requests and innocent spouse claims
- Prepare currently not collectible (CNC) status applications
- Request lien withdrawals and levy releases
- Prepare delinquent tax returns for clients with unfiled years
- Respond to IRS collection notices (CP501, CP503, CP504, etc.)
- Conduct power of attorney (Form 2848) submissions
- Communicate regularly with clients to update them on case status
- Maintain detailed case documentation and tracking
- Research complex tax code and IRS procedures
- Coordinate with tax preparers on client tax compliance

### Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
- Enrolled Agent (EA) certification or CPA license required
- 3-6 years of experience in US tax resolution or IRS representation
- Deep understanding of IRS Collection Procedures and Appeals process
- Experience with Offers in Compromise, installment agreements, and penalty abatement
- Strong knowledge of Collection Financial Standards and Form 433-A/433-B
- Excellent negotiation and communication skills
- Ability to analyze financial statements and tax transcripts
- Strong client service orientation
- Proficiency with IRS e-Services and CAF system

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience working with CPA firms or tax resolution companies in the USA
- Knowledge of state tax resolution procedures
- Familiarity with tax resolution software (Canopy, TaxDome, etc.)
- Experience with audit defense and Appeals procedures
- Previous experience training or mentoring junior staff
- Understanding of bankruptcy and tax debt implications

### What We Offer
- Competitive salary: ₹4,50,000 - ₹8,00,000 per annum based on experience and credentials
- Performance-based incentives
- Remote work flexibility
- Continuing education credits and professional development
- Access to professional tax research tools
- Health insurance coverage
- Opportunity to work on diverse and challenging cases
- Supportive team environment
- Career advancement opportunities as we expand

### Screening Questions
1. Describe your experience with IRS tax resolution cases. What types of cases have you handled (OIC, installment agreements, audit defense, etc.)?
2. Are you currently an Enrolled Agent or CPA? If EA, what is your PTIN number?
3. What is your success rate with Offer in Compromise submissions?
4. Describe a particularly challenging tax resolution case you handled and the outcome.
5. How do you stay current with changes to IRS collection procedures and policies?
6. What is your expected salary and notice period?
